The problem

Alabama HVAC owners are running two businesses: the field work and the paperwork.

The field work is what you're good at. The paperwork is what's killing you. Here's where the hours actually go.

Scheduling still runs through one person

One dispatcher, one phone, one bottleneck. When they're sick or slammed, everything backs up. AI can handle 80% of inbound scheduling without a human touching it.

Follow-up calls aren't happening

Every completed job is a potential maintenance contract or referral. Most HVAC companies follow up on maybe 20% of them because there's no system. There should be.

A tech finishes a site visit. The quote sits in someone's inbox for three days. The customer calls someone else. This is fixable with a 10-minute automated quote workflow.

Happy customers don't leave reviews unless you ask them at exactly the right moment. An automated post-service sequence fixes this — and Google rankings follow.
