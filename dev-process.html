01.

Free Project Assessment

We know that you are diligent in hiring quality fulltime engineers; because of this, we believe in proving our value as quickly as possible. In this meeting, we sit down and listen to what you are trying to achieve in your project and whiteboard solutions with you. We go over architecture, deployment, and scaling issues that will come up during the project's lifecycle. We do this by levering our engineers' experience delivered several projects from infancy to multi-million revenue-generating products.

02.

Discuss Project Financial Goals

We believe in the philosophy of all projects must either generate revenue or increase profit. Our senior engineers have led large engineering departments and understand how important it is to make the best use of resources. For this reason, we always have conversations with our customers to understand how the proposed project will help their business. This conversation allows us to look back once the project is complete to see if the project was a net positive for you. This approach enables us to build a long term relationship by demonstrating that we care about your business and your success.

03.

Dicuss Project Timeline 

What gets measured gets done. Getting large projects done on time and into our clients' hands is what we make our name on. For this reason, our development process relies heavily on quick iterations of hours and not days or weeks. Our intense focus on deadlines allows both you and our engineers to keep the focus on what matters the most to you.
