The problem

Every hour your PM spends on paperwork is an hour off the jobsite.

Construction margins are already thin. The admin overhead eating into them is largely automatable. Here's where the time actually goes.

📝

Daily reports take 45 minutes a day per superintendent

Weather, crew count, work completed, issues — the same information entered manually every afternoon. An AI tool can generate a draft from a 2-minute voice note.

🔄

Change orders live in email threads and text messages

By the time a change order is documented, someone's already done the work and you're arguing about who approved what. A structured workflow fixes this before it becomes a dispute.

👷

Subcontractor coordination is a full-time job

Scheduling, certifications, insurance docs, lien waivers — chasing subs for paperwork is something a well-designed system can handle automatically.

📁

Bid packages take too long to put together

Pulling scope sheets, specs, and pricing from past jobs to build a new bid shouldn't take a full day. AI can generate a first draft from your project history in minutes.

Change order workflow

Bid document generator
